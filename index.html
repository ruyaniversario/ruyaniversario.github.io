Experience

My most notable experience so far was as a chess player, I have played in lots of competition at other schools and I don't even remember some, open tournaments in some part of the province I currently live in.

MIMAROPA 2014

This was during February of 2014, at Boac, Marinduque. It was a great experience to me especially because I finish 1st in this competition.

Palarong Pambansa 2014

This was during May of 2014 I was so grateful during this time because this moment is one of a lifetime to me as a graduating student in highschool, to represent the province and my school in a national competition. Though, I finished nowhere near I still have a lot of reason to be happy.

STRASUC 2014

Just months after I compete in Palarong Pambansa 2014 I was given a chance once again to compete this time as a college student after I won the Intramural Meet and it was held during December of 2014 at UP Los Baños in Laguna, I was upset a little because I did not win but still happy because it's my first time playing in regionals as a college.

STRASUC 2015

This is my second time to reperesent my school in a regional meet during December of 2015 held at Aborlan, Palawan. This is a second upset to me just a little because I finish 3rd but not enough to make a push to nationals, It was still a great experience though.

STRASUC 2016 TO SCUAA 2017

For the third time I was able to continue my journey as a chess player, I represent my school once again in a regionals held on December of 2016 at Tanay, Rizal and this time with a great price. My overall performance was enough to make a push in to national competition as a college student, it was held on February of 2017 at Lipa, Batangas. It was a great feeling to me to participate in such competition even though I did not expect to finish near at the top here. It is still the most beautiful experience happened to me as a player in college.
